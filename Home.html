Hi! My name is Ryan Garrett and this is my portfolio page! I've been in the design field since late 2022, but have been doing art since I was little.

For my designs, I have made t-shirts, banners, flyers, posters, and sports books for schools like Bear River High School and Sunrise High School, alongside businesses like Kent's Market and some construction companies.

For my artwork, I have been creating a group of artworks named "SIMULATION". My style is combining glitch effects with a painterly style to create surrealistic, abstract pieces. I've been featured in four Downtown Logan Gallery Walks, have had my artwork displayed in Salt Lake City, had the opportunity to run a booth for my art at the 99th Box Elder County Fair in Tremonton, and now will have an upcoming art show in Ogden on June 5th!

As for my artist name: RyanitoArts, I got that nickname from one of my coworkers. Don't ask how, it just happened.
